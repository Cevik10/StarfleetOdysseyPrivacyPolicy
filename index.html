Privacy Policy

Starfleet Odyssey · Sci-Fi Command RPG

Effective date: June 14, 2026 · Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Agile Company ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the mobile application Starfleet Odyssey (the "App"), available on the Apple App Store.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information stored on your device

Starfleet Odyssey does not require you to create an account or sign in. Game progress is saved locally on your device in a file named user_progress.json. This may include:

  • Captain name and character preferences (gender, personality traits)
  • Story progress, choices, and completed chapters
  • In-game currency balances (Dilithium, Latinum)
  • Rank, crew, ship state, and owned cosmetic items
  • App settings (sound, animations, interface brightness, selected theme)
  • Onboarding and subscription status flags

This data stays on your device unless you delete the App or reset your device. We do not operate a cloud sync or backend server for game saves in the current version of the App.

Purchase information

If you make in-app purchases or subscribe to Premium, transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

We use RevenueCat (RevenueCat Privacy Policy) to manage subscriptions and consumable purchases. RevenueCat may receive:

  • An anonymous app user identifier
  • Purchase and subscription status
  • Product identifiers and transaction metadata
  • Device and app version information needed to validate purchases

Information we do not collect

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Your real name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact us directly)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, or microphone/camera content
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted advertising

The App does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs beyond RevenueCat for in-app purchase management.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Save and restore your game progress on your device
  • Deliver purchased content (Premium access, Dilithium packs, bundles)
  • Validate and restore App Store purchases
  • Improve stability and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests you send us

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Apple — payment processing and App Store infrastructure
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase validation
  • Legal authorities — if required by applicable law or to protect our rights

4. Data Retention

Local game data is retained on your device until you delete the App or remove the save file. RevenueCat and Apple retain purchase records according to their own policies.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Delete local data: Uninstall the App from your device, or delete app data through iOS Settings.
  • Restore purchases: Use the "Restore Purchases" button in the App (Supply tab or onboarding paywall).
  • Manage subscriptions: Open iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.
  • Contact us: Email us to request information about data we hold from support correspondence.

Depending on your region (including the EEA, UK, and California), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us using the details below.

6. Children's Privacy

Starfleet Odyssey is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed through the App and our service providers. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Local game data on your device is your responsibility to protect through your device passcode and backups.

8. International Users

The App is operated from Turkey. If you use the App from outside Turkey, your information may be processed in Turkey and in countries where Apple and RevenueCat operate, including the United States.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
